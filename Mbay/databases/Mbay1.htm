àdə̄
VT2   give
ādə̄-m̄ síndá-gá̰à̰ tə́ lò-í m-āw-ň mbāa.
Please give me your bicycle so I can travel with it.
VT   allow, permit
ādə̄ m-āw sè-í.
Let me go with you.
Súu gèe kàdə̄ Ngāró àdə̄-ǹ n-àw̄ sì-á.
Suu wants Ngaro to let him go with him.
VT   have, let
ādə̄ ndà ndɔ̄ɔ̄ dɔ̀-á tə́.
Have him pay a fine.
VT   leave for, leave to
Kó̰o̰-í sà dā̰a̰ túu-bè nà̰ àdə̄-jə̀ kə̄ngā tā̰á̰.
Your mother ate all the meat and left us only the bones.
Idioms
álà àdə̄ [dèē] God to save [s.o.] álà àdə̄-á ngá̰y àdə̄ bɔ̀ɔ̄-á ɗèē àĺ. God saved him so that his father didn't come.
