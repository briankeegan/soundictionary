à (Syn: è) INT particle marking an interrogative sentence (alternate to wà). Bà̰ý à? How goes it? ī-tò kàrī à? How are you?
à (Syn: ànḛ̄) CNJ general connector of two independant clauses; then; and so, and then, and. Ngōn-í ì ɓògɨ̀ kàá à ì ngè-yòo àĺ. Even if your child is a thief, at least he's not a sorcerer.
